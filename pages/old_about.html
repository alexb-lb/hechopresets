My name is Dan Hecho and I am glad to present you my new website, where you can see and purchase my exclusive presets for color correction using Adobe Lightroom.

These preset packages represent all my working experience in Adobe Lightroom with the release of the very first version 1.1!
There you can see the presets which have been created over many years (more than eight) in the process of working on real commercial and art-creative shootings, as well as presets which combined experience of many master classes and workshops in different countries of Europe and America from 2010 to 2015.

The main thrust of my work is creative art photography, fine art. Therefore, the main part of presets devoted to non-standard color correction proceeds from my author's vision of the color palette, removing excess colors and leaving the two basic colors, approaching a range of classical painting using muted colors.


 


But along with custom color correction packages I add many presets from real wedding and family shots, there are a more natural color with a nice bright skin tone.

For those who are interested to learn how to get warm-cold colors on the photos using my presets, I offer to watch a short video lesson, that will help you to learn master this technique easily and achieve the results that are similar to mine in your work.
